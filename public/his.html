History of the Matthayombanbangkapi Wind Symphony

Matthayom Banbangkapi Wind Symphonywas established in 2005. In 2006, Mr. Tewuth Simaseri became the conductor and instructor of the marching band. In 2013, Mr. Tastee Patumchat and Mr. Theerachai Chatchaiwattanakul took over as conductors and instructors. In 2023, the Matthayom Banbangkapi Wind Symphony underwent a transformation into a full-fledged concert band, becoming the Matthayombanbangkapi Winds Symphony. This development was made possible with the generous support of the Governor of Bangkok, Mr. Chadchart Sittipunt, who provided the band with musical instruments.
